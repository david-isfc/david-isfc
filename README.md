<h1 align="center">Embedded Systems & Robotics</h1>

<p align="center">
  <strong>Embedded Software · Real-Time Systems · Robotics & Automation</strong>
</p>

<p align="center">
  I focus on the design and implementation of embedded software for robotic and automated systems,
  with an emphasis on <strong>deterministic behavior</strong>, <strong>hardware-level control</strong>,
  and <strong>reliable communication under real-world constraints</strong>.
</p>

---

<h3>Research & Technical Interests</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Real-time embedded systems (bare-metal and RTOS-based)</li>
  <li>Embedded control for mobile and industrial robots</li>
  <li>Low-level communication protocols and timing-critical systems</li>
  <li>Sensor acquisition, signal decoding, and fault-tolerant design</li>
  <li>Integration of AI methods into resource-constrained embedded platforms</li>
</ul>

---

<h3>Selected Technical Work</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Design of a custom PWM-based communication protocol for mobile robots</li>
  <li>Implementation of multi-node robot ↔ base communication using UART and timers</li>
  <li>Decoding of inductive and wired signals using capture interrupts and ADC sampling</li>
  <li>Development of embedded safety mechanisms (emergency stop, system states)</li>
  <li>Hands-on control and automation projects with strict timing constraints</li>
</ul>
